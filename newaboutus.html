ABOUT US

History

.

Founded on May 18, 2019, was established with a commitment to providing comprehensive medical care for swine and other animals.

.

Between 2019 and 2023, we proudly extended our services to benefit 10 Barangays within the Tugbok District. Our Mission remains steadfast as we continue to pursue the expansion of our services to reach even more communities in the surrounding areas.




Mission


"Our mission is to offer you the finest in swine care services, tailored to your unique requirements."

Vision


"Transforming Swine Care for a Brighter Future in Agriculture."
